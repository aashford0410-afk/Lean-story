Chapter two

Then we moved.
And I fell off.

Life as a military family means you pivot fast. We moved, routines got disrupted, and somewhere in the chaos I stopped taking LEAN consistently.

About 10 lbs came back. And I noticed it: not just in my clothes, but in my confidence. In my energy. In how I showed up in photos.

You know that feeling when you're hiding behind someone in every picture? That was me that holiday season. Hiding behind my son. Smiling on the outside but missing who I'd been just months before.

"I missed the freedom. The confidence. The energy. I missed me."
